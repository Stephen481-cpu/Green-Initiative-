About Green Initiative

Green Initiative is a non-governmental organization headquartered in London. The organization is dedicated to promoting sustainable development, climate change mitigation, and environmental adaptation strategies worldwide.

Our Mission

To support paradigm shifts in climate mitigation and adaptation efforts while partnering with institutions to mobilize funding for climate action.

Our Vision

A world where communities thrive sustainably through climate resilience and environmental responsibility.

Core Values

  • Environmental Sustainability
  • Transparency & Accountability
  • Community Empowerment
  • Innovation & Partnership
